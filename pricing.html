Add-on Details

🌙 Understanding Overnight Care Premium

The overnight premium covers a complete overnight shift (typically 10pm–7am) where a fully awake caregiver remains on-site. This is not a sleeping shift — your caregiver is alert and active throughout the night.

The flat-rate premium of $120/night (or $140 on weekends) is charged in addition to any daytime hourly rates. It covers the caregiver's extended engagement, nighttime supplies, and a safety performance guarantee.

Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Is there a minimum booking period?

Most services have a 2-hour minimum. Post-Surgery and Dementia care require a 4-hour minimum due to the specialized preparation involved.

Are weekend rates automatic?

Yes, weekend rates are automatically applied when you select Saturday or Sunday as your booking date. You'll see this reflected clearly in your booking summary.

Can I combine multiple care types?

Yes! You can combine services. The rate applied will be based on the highest-tier service selected to ensure you receive the most qualified caregiver.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely after your booking is confirmed.
