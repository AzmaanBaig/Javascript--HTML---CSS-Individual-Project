Add-on Details

🌙 Understanding the Overnight Premium

The overnight premium covers a full overnight shift (10pm–7am) with a fully awake caregiver on-site. This is not a sleeping shift — your caregiver stays alert through the night.

The flat rate of $120/night (or $140 on weekends) is added on top of your daytime hourly rate. There are no extra hidden charges.

Common Questions

Pricing FAQs

Is there a minimum booking period?

Most services have a 2-hour minimum. Post-Surgery and Dementia care need a 4-hour minimum due to specialised preparation time.

Are weekend rates automatic?

Yes. Weekend rates are applied automatically when you select Saturday or Sunday. You'll see the difference clearly in your booking summary.

Can I combine multiple care types?

Yes! You can select multiple services. The rate applied will be the highest-tier service to ensure a suitably qualified caregiver is matched.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major cards, UPI, and bank transfers. Payment is processed securely after your booking is confirmed.
